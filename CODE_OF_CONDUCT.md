# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the Ferrite project and its community will experience an environment free of harassment and discrimination.

## Expected Behavior

We expect all participants to:

- **Be respectful** — Treat all community members with courtesy and respect.
- **Be inclusive** — Welcome people of all backgrounds, identities, and experiences.
- **Be collaborative** — Work together constructively to solve problems and improve the project.
- **Be professional** — Maintain a professional tone in all interactions, online and offline.
- **Assume good intent** — Interpret the words and actions of others charitably.
- **Be patient** — Remember that we're all learning. Help each other grow.

## Unacceptable Behavior

We do not tolerate:

- **Harassment** of any kind, including sexual harassment, discriminatory language, or intimidating conduct
- **Discrimination** based on race, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, age, religion, or any other protected characteristic
- **Bullying or shaming** — Deliberate attempts to embarrass, shame, or humiliate others
- **Trolling** — Intentionally provoking or upsetting others for entertainment
- **Spam** — Unsolicited advertising or off-topic content
- **Privacy violations** — Sharing others' personal information without consent
- **Violence or threats** — Any form of violence or threats of violence
- **Illegal conduct** — Any actions that violate local, state, or international law

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness a violation of this Code of Conduct:

1. **Report privately** to the maintainers at [conduct@ferrite.dev](mailto:conduct@ferrite.dev)
   - Include details of the incident
   - Include relevant context (links, dates, participants)
   - Explain how the incident affected you or others
2. **Do not** publicly shame or call out the person — this can escalate the situation
3. **Be specific** — The more details, the better we can help

All reports are taken seriously and handled with confidentiality and care.

## Investigation and Response

Upon receiving a report:

1. **Acknowledgment** — We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours
2. **Investigation** — We will investigate the incident promptly and fairly
3. **Communication** — We will keep you informed of the process (with appropriate confidentiality)
4. **Resolution** — We will take appropriate action, which may include:
   - A private conversation with the person involved
   - A public apology
   - Temporary or permanent removal from the community
   - Other remedial action as appropriate

We are committed to being transparent about the process while protecting the privacy of all parties.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All spaces within the Ferrite organization on GitHub (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- The official project Discord/chat community
- Project-related events and conferences
- Email communications related to the project
- Any other spaces explicitly designated as part of the Ferrite community

This Code of Conduct also applies to representatives of the project in public spaces. Examples include using an official project email address, posting via an official social media account, or acting as an official representative at a conference or online event.

## Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- **Warning** — A private communication about the violation and expectations for future behavior
- **Temporary suspension** — Removal from community spaces for a specified period (usually 7-30 days)
- **Permanent removal** — Indefinite removal from the community
- **Legal action** — In cases of serious violations, appropriate legal authorities may be involved

The maintainers will use their judgment to determine appropriate consequences based on the severity and context of the violation.

## Appeal Process

If you believe a decision was made in error or without due consideration:

1. Submit an appeal within 30 days to [conduct@ferrite.dev](mailto:conduct@ferrite.dev)
2. Include the relevant context and explain why you believe the decision should be reviewed
3. A different member of the Code of Conduct team will review your appeal
4. We will respond with a decision within 14 days
